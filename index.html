Manga Zip Tool

How to use?
  1. Choose a manga archive (.zip file).
  2. Perform the following operations:
    • Click on the pages you want to delete.
    • Drag and drop the pages you want to change order.
  3. Click 'Process Zip File', you will get a new zip with only the pages you want.
